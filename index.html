Build your space station

Have you ever wanted to build your own space station? Trade goods and care for the inhabitants? Explore distant galaxies and take on daring missions while trying to keep your meeple alive! Meeple station is an open-ended space station simulator in the vein of Rimworld that succeeds in doing just that. Live a never ending adventure whilst trying to make the best of your ramshackle station.

Co-op with friends

Play with your friends in cooperative mode. Perhaps you will undertake a campaign, build difficult stations and meet pressing deadlines. Or sit back, relax and let chaos reign supreme, running things at your own pace, moving around the quadrant, exploring nearby planets and being a centre for trade and commerce.






Satisfying simulation gameplay

Meeple Station was birthed from the love of two genres. The complex simulation genre, like Dwarf Fortress and Rimworld and good ol' real-time strategy games, like Age of Empires and Starcraft.
